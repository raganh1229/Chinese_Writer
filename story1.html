Chinese Grammar Part 1

2018年 08月 15日

1.）辆 – Measure word for vehicles.
Ex.) 我有两辆车。
I have two cars.
Ex.) 我觉得他的三辆车非常漂亮。
I think his three cars are beautiful.


2.）比较 + adj. / A + 跟 + B + 相比较 – Comparing two objects and providing an opinion.
Ex.）我觉得你比较聪明。
I think you are relatively smart.
Ex.) 我觉得他的意见跟我的意见相比较更正确。
His opinion, compared to my opinion, is relatively more correct.


3.）省 + 什么什么 + 钱 – Save money.
4.）存 + 什么什么 + 钱/时候 – Save money or time.
Ex.) 因为我吃不太贵的饭所以我省很多钱。
Because I eat less expensive meals, I save a lot of money.
Ex.) 我喜欢存很多钱所以我可以旅行。
I really like to save a lot of money, so I can travel.
Ex.) 我存了很多时候。


5.) A + 不见得 + B + adj. – Not necessarily/unlikely.
Ex.) 虽然他考得很好但是他口语的汉语不见得比你的好。
Although he did well in the exam, his spoken Chinese is not necessarily as good as yours.
Ex.) 因为他没有意见的很好他们不见得觉得他是正确。
Because he has no good opinions, they don’t necessarily think he is correct.


6.) A + 对 + B + 有 + 好处/坏处 – Good and negative effects.
Ex.) 抽烟对你的身体有坏处。
Smoking has a bad effect on your body.
Ex.) 交朋友对你的生活有好处。
Making friends is good for your life.


7.) A + 对 + B + 很/不太 + 适应 – To adapt.
Ex.) 我对上海的天气不太适应。
I have not adapted to Shanghai’s climate.
Ex.) 他对上海交通大学的校园生活很适应。
He has adapted to Shanghai Jiao Tong’s academic life.
