Hi. My name is Ragan, John Ragan to be exact - but please, call me JR. I began studying Chinese during my junior year of high school and have carried my love and passion for the language into my college career. In my free time, I enjoy hanging out with friends, designing websites, and practicing Python, C, and C++. While my current academic path is electrical and computer engineering, I plan on using the latter degree to focus on a career dedicated to video game development.
