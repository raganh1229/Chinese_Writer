Week 4: Golden

September 28th - October 7th, commonly known as China's Golden Week, is a designated set of national holidays where millions of Chinese people travel the country to view provincial attractions. Together, alongside my pals Carlos, Aschraf, and Erik, we took advantage of our time off to visit four major cities in eastern China: Qufu, Tai'an, Jinan, and Beijing. These pictures catalog our journey.

Weeks 1-3: Intro

My first few weeks in China were unlike any other in my entire life. I was taken out of the comfort zone that my home country provided and quickly learned to adapt to a culture I'd always believed to be so different from my own. However, as I got acquainted with SJTU and Shanghai as a whole, I began to grow more comfortable in my new environment and even came to realize that the United States and China aren't so different after all.
